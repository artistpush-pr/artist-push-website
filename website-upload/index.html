Refund Policy

1. Our Guarantee

At Artist Push, we stand behind the quality of our music promotion services. We are committed to delivering every order in full, on time, and as described. If we fail to meet these standards, we offer a straightforward refund process to make it right.

2. Eligibility for Refund

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund in the following cases:

  • Non-delivery: If your order has not started within 5 business days of placement and we cannot provide a resolution
  • Partial delivery: If we are unable to deliver the full quantity ordered, you will receive a proportional refund for the undelivered portion
  • Duplicate orders: If you accidentally placed the same order twice, the duplicate will be refunded in full
  • Service error: If we delivered the wrong service type or applied promotion to the wrong track/profile

3. Refund Not Applicable

Refunds are generally not available in these situations:

  • Order already delivered: Once the full quantity has been delivered and confirmed, the order is considered complete
  • Natural drop-off: Minor fluctuations in follower/play counts are normal on all streaming platforms and are not grounds for a refund. We provide reasonable over-delivery to account for this.
  • Incorrect links: If you provided an incorrect or private Spotify/SoundCloud URL and the order was processed based on the link given
  • Account changes: If you deleted, privated, or significantly changed the track/profile during active delivery
  • Change of mind: If delivery has already begun, we cannot issue a refund simply because you no longer want the service
  • Platform actions: If a streaming platform removes content or takes action on your account for reasons unrelated to our service

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@artistpush.net with the subject line "Refund Request"
  • Include your order number, the email used for the order, and the track/profile URL
  • Describe the issue in detail (e.g., non-delivery, wrong service, partial delivery)

5. Processing Time

Refund requests are reviewed within 2–3 business days. If approved:

  • Credit/debit card refunds are processed via Stripe and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank
  • Cryptocurrency refunds will be issued to the original wallet address at the current exchange rate

6. Partial Refunds and Credits

In some cases, we may offer a partial refund or account credit instead of a full refund. This typically applies when a significant portion of the order has been delivered. We may also offer to re-deliver the remaining quantity as an alternative to a monetary refund.

7. Dispute Resolution

If you are unsatisfied with our refund decision, you may request a secondary review by emailing us with additional details. We aim to resolve all disputes fairly and promptly. Filing a chargeback with your bank before contacting us may result in delays and additional fees.

8. Contact

Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience. Cookies are widely used across the internet and are essential for many website features.

2. How We Use Cookies

Artist Push uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies (Required)

  • Maintaining your session while browsing our website
  • Processing orders and managing cart functionality
  • Ensuring website security and fraud prevention

Functional Cookies

  • Remembering your preferences (e.g., selected service types, currency)
  • Storing your recently viewed services for convenience
  • Keeping you logged in across sessions

Analytics Cookies

  • Understanding how visitors use our website (pages visited, time spent, bounce rate)
  • Identifying popular services and content
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • We use analytics tools that aggregate and anonymize data where possible

Marketing Cookies

  • Delivering relevant advertisements on third-party platforms
  • Tracking conversions from our ad campaigns
  • Limiting the number of times you see a specific ad

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. These include:

  • Stripe: For secure payment processing
  • Google Analytics: For website usage analysis
  • Social media platforms: If you interact with embedded social features

We do not control how third parties use their cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Note: Disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality, including the ability to place orders or use the cart.

5. Cookie Retention

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain for 30 days to 12 months depending on the cookie's purpose
  • Analytics cookies: Typically retained for up to 12 months

6. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. We currently do not respond to DNT signals, but we respect your privacy choices and provide cookie management options as described above.
