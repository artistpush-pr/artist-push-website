Spotify-Specific Questions

Will Spotify plays count toward my royalties?

Our Premium Royalty Plays service is specifically designed to generate royalty-eligible streams. Standard plays focus on boosting your visible stream count and algorithmic signals. Check the service description for each play type to see royalty eligibility details.

Can playlist placement help my algorithmic reach?

Yes. When your track is placed in active playlists with engaged listeners, Spotify's algorithm recognizes the increased engagement (plays, saves, completion rate) and is more likely to recommend your music in Discover Weekly, Release Radar, and related artist radio stations.

What is the difference between plays, saves, and monthly listeners?

Plays increase your total stream count for a specific track. Saves (library adds) signal to Spotify that listeners want to hear your music again, which strongly influences algorithmic recommendations. Monthly listeners reflect the total number of unique users who played your music in the last 28 days — a key metric for labels, playlist curators, and booking agents.

SoundCloud-Specific Questions

Do SoundCloud plays come from real accounts?

Yes. Our SoundCloud promotion uses real accounts with genuine listening activity. We never use bots or fake profiles. The engagement patterns look natural and contribute to your track's visibility in SoundCloud's trending charts and genre feeds.

Can reposts help my track trend on SoundCloud?

Absolutely. Reposts are one of the strongest engagement signals on SoundCloud. When your track gets reposted by active accounts, it appears in the feeds of their followers, creating a chain reaction of organic discovery. Combined with plays and likes, reposts can push your track into SoundCloud's trending charts.

What is the Organic Pack and how does it work?

The Organic Pack combines plays, likes, and followers into a single bundle designed to create a natural-looking engagement pattern. Instead of boosting just one metric, the pack raises all key signals simultaneously — the way real viral growth looks. This makes your promotion harder to distinguish from genuine organic traction.
